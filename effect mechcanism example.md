Status Effects
Status Effects are effects that will affect enemies or towers in a certain way, this will add more aspects into the games difficulty and fun factor. These Status Effects can affect towers and enemies in both positive and negative way, which will also add variety into gameplay.

Most Status Effects will be applied when the enemies spawn, though some of them will be applied upon an ability of another enemy or certain circumstances, each status effect will be somewhat different and unique from each other, and some of them can stack and others can't.

Enemy Status Effects
Prolific Growth
Causes uncontrollable growth on enemies, can be visually seen by tumors growing on these enemies, the tumors are abnormal growth from enemies, usually seen by extra/excess muscles or tissue on the enemy, Prolific Growth will damage some of the nerves the enemies has, making them slightly more aggressive towards their enemies, Prolific Growth is especially dangerous as they will heal damaged tissues and muscles rapidly.

Cannot Stack with Resistance.

"2-5 HP/Second" For example, means the statistics will be changed depending by the strength of the enemy.
Aggressive Attack Buffs only applies on enemies that has offensive abilities.


Resistance
Resistance causes enemies to be more resilient against attacks in general, some specific type of attacks may reduce the effects caused by Resistance, It works by evolving muscles and tissues of the enemy, it will also cause enemies to take more of a defensive stance when attacked. Resistance is quite dangerous due to its ability to Regenerate Defense. This effect can be seen by its Blue Aura.

Cannot Stack with Prolific Growth.

Resistance will not apply on bosses
Battle Surge
Battle Surge is an effect caused by Major Bosses or an status effect temporarily given to enemies to boost their advance, Battle Surge is extremely dangerous as they are capable of increasing the speed and attack of enemies by boosting their morale, Battle Surge will apply for around 2-5 seconds. Though in this duration, the enemy is able to deal sufficient blow to your base, this effect can be seen by its Yellow Aura.

Battle Surge may be applied to nearby enemies when a Boss does an attack or an ability.

Speed Buff	Offensive Buffs	Defensive Buffs	Other Buffs
125%	+3 Second Stun, 150% Unit Damage, -1 Second Ability Cooldown	1 Second Invincibility at the Start, 5% Defense	Punch
Notes

Punch is an ability added to normal enemies with no offensive abilities, they will stun for 1 second upon hitting a tower.
Major Bosses cannot be affected by the Battle Surge.
Crystalized
Crystalized causes the enemy to partially Crystalize, this effect will reduce the enemy mobility, though it will make it stronger and partially or completely immune to certain types of attacks, though some of it limbs would need to be sacrificed and replaced by the crystal. Crystalized enemies are especially dangerous due to their defense count being high.

Crystalized effect cannot stack with other effects.

Notes

Partial Immunity means damage dealt by that tower will be decreased by 50%.
Explosion Towers and the Ranger is the only towers that can damage an enemy with this effect.
Frostbite
Frostbite causes the enemy to freeze with low temperatures, which hurts and incapacitate enemies, reducing their health and lowering their defense. Frostbite causes damage during a prolonged amount of time, Frostbite effect will disappear after the duration runs out.

Anger
Anger indicates that the enemy is extremely hostile and aggressive, and will attack nearby towers when given the chance, and will increase the strength of the enemy temporarily, though this will result in some negative effects.

When stacked with Battle Surge, the added stun duration will be decreased by 50% (1.5 Seconds).

Defeated
Once the major boss of the wave has been defeated, all enemies that are from the same wave will be given this effect until they die. This resembles somewhat of a dying wish or a last attempt at trying to defeat the enemy despite low morals, sometimes this effect can span multiple wave depending on the gamemode.

Tower Status Effects
Firerate Boost
Boosts the tower's firerate, depends on the percentage given to the tower.

Range Boost
Boosts the tower's range, depends on the percentage given to the tower.

Discount
Decreases the price of tower upgrades, depends on the percentage given to the tower.

Firerate Debuff
Makes towers shoot slower by affecting the tower's firerate.

Stunned
Disables towers from shooting or attacking enemies, affected towers will be unable to attack for a set duration.

Rage
Increases the damage towers deal to enemies
