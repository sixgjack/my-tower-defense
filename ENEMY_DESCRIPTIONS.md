# Enemy Descriptions (Clash Royale Style)

This file contains Clash Royale-style descriptions for all enemies. These descriptions can be used for:
- Tutorial/help system
- Enemy info panels
- Tooltips during gameplay
- Future UI enhancements

## Basic Enemies

### Bug
**HP:** Low | **Speed:** Fast | **Reward:** Low
*Tiny but quick! These annoying critters swarm in large numbers. Don't let them overwhelm you.*

### Spider
**HP:** Low | **Speed:** Very Fast | **Reward:** Low
*Uses camouflage to blend in! Hard to spot but weak. A few good hits will take them down.*

### Mite
**HP:** Very Low | **Speed:** Very Fast | **Reward:** Very Low
*The smallest and fastest of the swarm! Weak individually but dangerous in groups.*

### Fly
**HP:** Very Low | **Speed:** Extremely Fast | **Reward:** Very Low
*Flying enemies that bypass ground defenses! Fast but fragile. Air defense recommended.*
